Indian Matchmaking is a Netflix original series that follows Mumbai-based matchmaker Sima Taparia as she works to find appropriate matches for her clients in India and the United States. The series provides an inside look at the arranged marriage process in India, as well as the challenges that can come with it. It also explores the role of matchmakers in India's modern society.
